This is simple password menager.
It can store your passwords in json file.
You always can recall your password by inputing website and clicking on "search" button.
It can generate random passwords with upper and lower cases, numbers and special signs. After generating, password is copied to clipboard and it's ready to paste on website of your choice. (This functionality may not work properly on linux, but it is easy to fix: https://pyperclip.readthedocs.io/en/latest/index.html#not-implemented-error).
